Description


This clever gadget cleans both sides of
a window at once using strong magnets. Just place one
part on the inside and the other on the outside — slide
it around and both sides get wiped clean. Perfect for
hard-to-reach windows in apartments or upper floors.

Why is it sustainable?


Reduces the need for disposable wipes
and chemical sprays. Most versions are reusable, durable,
and often made from recyclable plastic or non-toxic
materials — promoting longer-term cleaning solutions.
